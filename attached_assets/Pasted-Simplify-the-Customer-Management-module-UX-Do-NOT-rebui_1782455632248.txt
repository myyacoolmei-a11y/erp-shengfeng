Simplify the Customer Management module UX.

Do NOT rebuild the ERP.
Do NOT delete existing data.
Do NOT modify unrelated modules.
Keep the current Sheng Feng ERP style.

Goal:
Make customer management simple, fast, and suitable for mobile use.
Reduce unnecessary clicks.

Current problem:
Users must click the eye/view button to enter the customer detail page, then click again to edit or check related records.
This is too many steps.

Please change the Customer List page:

1. Remove the eye/view button from the customer list.

2. On each customer row, show clear direct action buttons:
- Edit
- Quotations
- Work Orders
- Payments
- Warranty
- Delete

3. Clicking the customer name should open Edit Customer directly.

4. Edit button:
Open customer edit modal directly from the list.

5. Quotations button:
Navigate to Quotation Management filtered by this customer.
Do not edit quotations inside Customer Detail.

6. Work Orders button:
Navigate to Work Order Management filtered by this customer.
Do not edit work orders inside Customer Detail.

7. Payments button:
Navigate to Payment Management filtered by this customer.

8. Warranty button:
Navigate to Warranty Management filtered by this customer.

9. Delete button:
Ask for confirmation.
Do not allow deleting a customer with linked quotations, work orders, payments, or warranty records.
Show a clear warning if deletion is blocked.

10. Customer Detail page:
Keep it only as optional read-only history page if needed, but do not make it the main workflow.
The main workflow should happen directly from the customer list.

11. Mobile UX:
On mobile, show action buttons as compact icons with labels if space allows.
Important actions should be reachable with one tap.

12. Search:
Keep the customer search bar.
Allow searching by:
- Customer name
- Phone
- Address
- Company name

13. Test:
- Edit customer directly from the list.
- Click customer name to edit.
- Go to quotations filtered by customer.
- Go to work orders filtered by customer.
- Go to payments filtered by customer.
- Delete customer with confirmation.
- Verify mobile layout works.
